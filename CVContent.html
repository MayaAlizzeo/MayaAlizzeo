Background and Family:

I was born and raised in Meadville, Pennsylvania by my parents Kinta and Gary Alizzeo. I have one sibling, an older sister named Sierra. She is three years older than me and also attended Elon University! We have two pets at home, a dog named Brady, and a cat named Domino.

Dance Experience:

I started dancing when I was three years old and immediately fell in love with it. I trained in ballet, pointe, modern, lyrical, tap, and jazz. When I was 11, I auditioned for a ballet company and ended up staying in it for seven years. I was every postion possible in the Oil Region Ballet Company from petite apprentice to principal ballerina. I didn't want to give up dance when I got to school, so I decided to become a dance minor here!
